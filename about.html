The Gun Store

About

The Gun Store (“TGS”) is a world famous gun range that was established in 1988. TGS was the first gun range in Las Vegas and after 30 years plus of tradition and unparalleled atmosphere, we continue to thrive to provide our clients with the best customer service and experience in the Las Vegas valley. We pride ourselves in maintaining excellent levels of safety while ensuring each customer has the time of their life. Effective February 1st, 2019 transfers will not be accepted without prior authorization. Call 702-454-1110 to obtain prior authorization.

The World Famous Gun Store has been Las Vegas’ premier indoor shooting range for over 25 years, catering to locals and tourists from all over the globe! With our range expansion project completed, we now offer twice as many air-conditioned lanes to shoot in (30), featuring 2 new large VIP Ranges with 6 lanes in each. We have more than 60 types of firearms to choose from, including full automatic machine guns, semi-automatic rifles, handguns, and shotguns for your shooting pleasures. Guests can also choose from a wide variety of targets to take aim at, ranging from your standard bull’s-eye and silhouette, on up to our very popular zombies, terrorists and hostage scenarios. Range Safety Instructors are provided for all of our shooters. Our staff is friendly and knowledgeable, and happy to answer all of your firearms questions. We offer a variety of classes, including Concealed Firearms Permit and Renewal, Ladies Only Concealed Firearm Permit Courses, and FREE Children’s Gun Safety.
